# **App Name**: Global Gateway Accounting

## Core Features:

- Service Showcase: Display the range of accounting and concierge services offered, such as business incorporation, financial accounting, international advising and concierge travel planning.
- About Section: Provide a detailed 'About Me' section to build trust and credibility with potential clients, highlighting expertise and experience.
- Contact Form: Enable potential clients to directly contact the accountant for inquiries or to schedule a consultation.
- Testimonials: Allow clients to upload a review after the service.
- Concierge travel plans: Let the AI build the best concierge travel plans for specific clients by suggesting the safest, cheapest, most convenient or most desirable locations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#224569) to convey professionalism and trust. Dark color scheme.
- Background color: Light grey (#E0E5EB), a desaturated version of the primary blue, for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Gold (#B8860B), an analogous color to the primary, to highlight important information.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif font to capture the spirit of high-end elegance; body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif font that offers great legibility and neutrality when paired with Playfair.
- Use elegant and simple icons that are related to accounting and finance. 
- A clean and well-structured layout, similar to the attached design, with clear sections for services, 'About Me,' and contact information. 
- Subtle animations (like fading or sliding) for a polished user experience when navigating the site.
